STAR WARS APP

Made by Zoran Karbev

A long time ago in a galaxy far, far, far away... It is a period of civil war.

Rebel Angular spaceships, striking from a hidden base, have won their first victory against the evil React Empire.

During the battle, many Angular spies managed to steal secret plans of the DEATH STAR, the React Empire’s ultimate weapon, an armored space station with enough power to destroy an entire planet.

Pursued by the React Empire’s sinister agents, Princess Leia races home aboard her starship, custodian of the stolen plans that can save her people and restore freedom to the galaxy….
